### The emotional architecture layer
the Vens refer to:

- world state
    
- emotional architecture
emotions aren’t just **internal variables**, but part of the **environmental field**.

So agents might react to things like:

- tension in the region
    
- loyalty networks
    
- unresolved conflicts
    
- social pressure
    

Which means conflict isn’t random — it emerges from **shared emotional structures**.

 closer to **social simulation** than typical NPC systems.
