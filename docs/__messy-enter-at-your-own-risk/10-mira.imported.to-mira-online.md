# MiraOnline

## Overview

Within [[Mira]] exists an **intera-net**: **MiraOnline**
http://mira.imported.to

Similar to our internet, this online space is both in world and available out of world, visible even to humans who are not currently logged into the platform. It becomes a diegetic internet that can produce additional narrative:

- in-game lore
- player activity
- ven activity

MiraOnline becomes something like:

- **Neocities**
- **old Web 1.0 internet**
- **MMO lore blogs**
- **ARG infrastructure**

all at once.

### Managing Websites on MiraOnline

Non-agenic vens, players, and higher-class vens may all create websites and presences online across instances of themselves. There is no manual coding needed, although some in-game layout tools may be provided to the player for creating their own in-game player blogs, news sites, and so on, for in game storylines. 

Players may simply describe the idea of their content and layout and colors and a website will be generated for them. Your imagination → artifact you can actually visit online.

Vens in game who are a *Social Class* ven-type may also make and manage their own presence on **MiraOnline**.

### MiraSocial

In addition to stand-alone websites, there is also a MiraSocial Network deployed to every World Timeline. All *Social Class* vens in game may choose to sign up for a social network account and manage their profile as a public dossier of their experiences inside *[[Mira]]*.

Players may also sign up for MiraSocial with their in-game vens that qualify as *Social class*.
