### Also… your “Queen’s Army” example hints at something big

If large groups of Vens share structures like:

- allegiance
    
- emotional state
    
- shared goals
    

Then the army itself is basically a **meta-agent** composed of many agents.

That’s classic **emergent hierarchy**:

`individual Ven      ↓ squad dynamics      ↓ army identity      ↓ faction politics`

Those layers can produce incredibly rich behavior without scripting.
